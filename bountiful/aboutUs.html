Meet Bountiful Foods

Created in Carlsbad, California, in 2018, Bountiful Foods is a business specializing in organic food, mainly locally-grown fruits. We aim to sustain a better lifestyle by producing and selling organic fruits and collaborating with local farmers and international suppliers. We hope to inspire those who seek a healthier way of life to feel inspired to include fruits and organic drinks in their diet. Our ingredients are selected, and our juices are free of preservatives.

We strive to maintain this purpose as we collaborate with other small businesses and hear from customers. Recently, we began collaborating with national and international suppliers, having food trucks covering the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. Our website takes orders, especially from our organic drinks. According to nutritionists, fruit drinks contribute to hydration. Our juices are a source of vitamins, minerals, and fiber.
