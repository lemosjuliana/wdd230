Target Audience

The target audience consists of business owners, leaders, or any organization that would conduct or start business relations with the city of Feira de Santana, Brazil.

Persona - Supermarket franchise owner

Name - Daniel Reale

Job Title - Executive Director

Demographics

  • 48 years old
  • Married
  • Father of four children
  • Has a post degree in Business and Economic

Goals and tasks - Daniel plays a strong leadership role and seeks to participate the most he can in every process, reviewing reports, promoting training, and strengthening business relations.

Environment - Daniel is familiar with computer use and navigation but often uses his cell phone for a quick search. Part of his work requires him to be on his computer constantly.

Quote - "Nice approach! Can we talk about it on Monday? I would like to hear more."

Persona - Software Company Manager

Name - Filipe Soares

Job Title - Senior Manager

Demographics

  • 52 years old
  • Married
  • Father of two children
  • Has a doctorate in Logistics

Goals and tasks - Filipe is responsible for analyzing data and providing changes and new solutions according to them. To focus on his main task, Filipe delegates others to participate in the business process, creating a collaborators net.

Environment - Filipe is very familiar with computer navigation. He utilizes a computer connected to the internet as his favorite job tool.

Quote - "I will review the reports and provide enough data to make the needed change."
