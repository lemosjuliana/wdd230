Feira's city hall. The city is politically emancipated since 1833

The growing pace of development of the village required the construction of wider roads, and the commercial houses began to be installed to serve the growing population. Today the population is over 600,000 inhabitants (2020 Census). The construction of the city hall began in 1921, by Colonel Bernardino da Silva Bahia, and it was completed in 1926, by Colonel Arnold Silva. The building holds the Mayor's Office, the Agriculture Department, the Environment and Natural Resources Department, the Social Government Department and the Main Hall.

The commerce is the most important economic activity in Feira

Feira de Santana is one of the most important economic centers in the state. The city's economy supports itself through the service sector and the trade sectors. Together, they are responsible for 63% of the city's production. Over time, other segments stood out, such as industrial activities. There is no way of measuring the importance of the city's economy for the region, the state, and for the whole of country. Because of that, Feira is expanding.
