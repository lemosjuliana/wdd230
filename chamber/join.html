Membership Level

NP Membership

NP Membership is for non-profit organizations, and there is no fee. If the institution is accountable for this membership, we offer a 10% off discount for every Chamber partner and event. Check out our newest member: Hemoba Family Hospital.

Bronze Membership

The Bronze Membership offers 10% off discounts in events with the Chamber seal and level 3 priority in partnerships. Besides that, the members gain visibility when advertising their commerce or business. Check out some of our members: Mundo Veterin√°rio veterinary, and Chiquinho's Ice Cream.

Silver Membership

Besides the visibility when advertising, the Silver members have 15% off discounts in events with the Chamber seal and level 2 priority in partnerships. They count on free business assessorship for the first year of membership and a 5% off discount on the second year. Check out some of our members: Kadan Sushi, Ikebana flower shop, and Motopel dealership.

Gold Membership

Besides visibility when advertising, the Gold members have 20% off discounts in events with the Chamber seal, level 1 priority in partnerships, and a 5% off discount for every Chamber partner. They count on free business assessorship for the first two years of membership and a 5% off discount on the third year. Check out some of our members: Le Biscuit, Lojas Americanas and Boulevard Shopping.
