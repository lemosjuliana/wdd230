Welcome to Feira de Santana!

We aim to strengthen and develop business relations between professionals, leaders, and the city of Feira de Santana. The city locates in the metropolitan region and has a close history with commerce, developing around local fairs. We strive to maintain this tradition as we welcome new ideas, projects, and dreams.
